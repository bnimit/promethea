## Becoming a maintainer

The general path:

1. Demonstrate sustained, high-quality contribution to a subsystem (typically 6+ months, 20+ merged PRs of meaningful scope, plus reviews of others' work).
2. Be nominated by an existing subsystem lead.
3. The subsystem lead opens an RFC proposing the addition.
4. Other maintainers comment publicly. The subsystem lead records the disposition.

Stepping down: maintainers may step down at any time. They are encouraged to nominate a successor. Inactive maintainers (no contribution for 6 months) are moved to "Emeritus" by the Board.

## Emeritus

Past maintainers retain credit and gratitude. They retain commit signing privileges for any historical commits they authored but lose merge access.
